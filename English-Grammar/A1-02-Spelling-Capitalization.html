Capitalization Rules

When to Use Capital Letters

1. First word of a sentence

The cat is sleeping.

2. Names of people and places

John lives in London.

3. Days, months, and holidays

Monday, January, Christmas

4. The word "I"

Yesterday I went to the park.

Common Spelling Rules

Important Patterns

Drop the e before -ing

make → making

write → writing

Change y to i before -es

baby → babies

city → cities

Capitalization Examples

my name is sarah.

My name is Sarah.

i live in new york.

I live in New York.

we visit grandma every sunday.

We visit Grandma every Sunday.

my birthday is in july.

My birthday is in July.

Spelling Examples

makeing → making

writeing → writing

babys → babies

citys → cities

A Letter to a Friend

dear maria,

i hope you are well. last monday, i visited london with my family. we saw big ben and buckingham palace. it was amazing! we also went to a restaurant on oxford street. i want to visit paris next summer. do you want to come with me?

your friend,
john

Reading: Corrected Version

Here is the corrected text with proper capitalization:

Dear Maria,

I hope you are well. Last Monday, I visited London with my family. We saw Big Ben and Buckingham Palace. It was amazing! We also went to a restaurant on Oxford Street. I want to visit Paris next summer. Do you want to come with me?

Your friend,
John
