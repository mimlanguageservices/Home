Part 4: Write About the Photo

Describe each photo in detail. On the real DET you have 1 minute per photo. Aim for at least 50 words. Focus on what you can see — people, actions, objects, colors, setting. After submitting, review the rubric to evaluate your response.

Question 18
Write About the Photo — Open Response
📷 Photo Description: A group of people are gathered in a park on a sunny day. Some are sitting on a picnic blanket on the grass, others are standing near a barbecue grill. There are trees in the background. People appear to be laughing and enjoying food and drinks together.
Photo: Group gathering in a park
0 words
Self-Scoring Rubric (DET criteria):
✦ Excellent (4): Detailed description of multiple visual elements (people's actions, setting, objects, atmosphere). Varied sentence structure. Natural collocations (e.g., "gathered around," "bathed in sunlight"). 50+ words.
✦ Good (3): Covers main elements, minor errors only. 35–50 words.
✦ Fair (2): Basic details, limited vocabulary, some errors affect clarity. 20–35 words.
✦ Needs Work (1): Under 20 words or difficult to understand.

Sample strong response: "The photo shows a lively outdoor gathering in a green park on a warm, sunny day. A group of people — including adults and possibly children — are relaxing on a picnic blanket spread across the grass. Nearby, someone is tending to a barbecue grill. The atmosphere appears relaxed and joyful, with people chatting and laughing. Trees provide shade in the background."
Question 19
Write About the Photo — Open Response
📷 Photo Description: A modern, open-plan office. Several people are seated at large desks working on computers. The space has high ceilings with exposed pipes and natural light coming from large windows. There are plants and meeting pods visible in the background.
Photo: Modern open-plan office
0 words
Self-Scoring Rubric (DET criteria):
✦ Excellent (4): Uses precise workplace vocabulary (e.g., "open-plan," "workstations," "collaborate"). Describes layout, people, and atmosphere. 50+ words.
✦ Good (3): Identifies the setting and main activities with minor errors. 35–50 words.
✦ Fair (2): Very basic description. 20–35 words.
✦ Needs Work (1): Under 20 words or hard to understand.

Sample strong response: "The photo depicts a bright, modern open-plan office. Multiple employees are seated at large shared workstations, each focused on their computer screens. The space features high ceilings with industrial-style exposed pipes and large windows that allow natural light to fill the room. In the background, there are several green plants and what appear to be small meeting areas or pods. The overall atmosphere feels productive and contemporary."
