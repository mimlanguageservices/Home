Part 4: Write About the Photo

Look at each photo and write a detailed description in English. On the real DET you have 1 minute per photo. Aim for at least 50 words. Focus on what you see, not what you think about the topic. After submitting, review the scoring rubric to self-evaluate.

Question 18
Write About the Photo — Open Response
📷 Photo Description: A busy outdoor market scene. Colorful fruit and vegetable stalls line a narrow street. Shoppers browse the goods; vendors stand behind their displays. Some stalls have striped awnings. The street is crowded with people of different ages.
Photo: Outdoor market scene
0 words
Self-Scoring Rubric (DET criteria):
✦ Excellent (4): Detailed, specific description of multiple elements (people, objects, setting, actions). Natural vocabulary. Grammar mostly accurate. 50+ words.
✦ Good (3): Covers main elements with some detail. Minor grammar errors. 35–50 words.
✦ Fair (2): Basic description, limited vocabulary, some errors affect clarity. 20–35 words.
✦ Needs Work (1): Very short or unclear. Under 20 words or hard to understand.

Sample strong response: "The photo shows a lively outdoor market on a sunny day. Rows of colorful stalls display fresh fruits and vegetables. In the foreground, a vendor in a striped apron arranges oranges. Shoppers crowd the narrow lane; some carry bags already full of produce. The scene feels busy and vibrant."
Question 19
Write About the Photo — Open Response
📷 Photo Description: A young woman sits at a wooden desk. There are several open textbooks and notebooks around her. A laptop is open in front of her. She is writing in a notebook and appears focused. There is a cup of coffee or tea beside her.
Photo: Student studying at a desk
0 words
Self-Scoring Rubric (DET criteria):
✦ Excellent (4): Vivid description using academic vocabulary (e.g., "concentrated," "surrounded by," "illuminated by"). Clear sentence structure. 50+ words.
✦ Good (3): Describes main elements with minor errors. 35–50 words.
✦ Fair (2): Basic details only. 20–35 words.
✦ Needs Work (1): Under 20 words or unclear.

Sample strong response: "The photo shows a young woman studying at a desk. She is surrounded by open textbooks and notebooks and appears completely focused. Her laptop is open in front of her, and she is taking notes by hand. A warm drink sits beside her. The setting looks like a home or library study area."
